Underground Fire Line Repair & Replacement

Underground fire lines deteriorate over time from corrosion, soil movement, freeze-thaw cycles, construction activity, age, and improper installation. We repair and replace fire service lines, fire water mains, and fire hydrant supply lines, and we handle full fire line replacement when repeated repairs no longer make sense. Our team serves property owners, engineers, contractors, and municipalities across the Front Range.

Common Signs of a Fire Line Leak

  • Unexplained water usage or rising utility costs
  • Pressure loss during system testing
  • Wet areas, soft ground, or sinkholes on the property
  • Frequent fire pump cycling
  • Failed fire protection inspections
  • Visible drops in fire system performance

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does a fire line leak need urgent attention?
A leaking or low-pressure fire line can leave your sprinkler system or hydrants unable to perform during an emergency, and it can put you out of compliance with fire protection requirements. Prompt repair protects both safety and your standing with inspectors and insurers.
Can you find the leak without excavating the whole line?
Yes. We use acoustic leak detection, pressure testing, and utility locating to pinpoint the damaged section first, so excavation is limited to where the actual problem is.
Do you work with fire protection contractors and inspectors?
We do. Underground fire line repairs often involve coordination with engineers, fire protection professionals, municipalities, and inspectors. We manage the underground utility work so the overall system returns to compliant, reliable operation.
