Residential Water Line Repair

The water service line connecting your home to the municipal main is one of the most important parts of your plumbing. Over time it can fail from age, corrosion, shifting soil, tree root intrusion, or freezing temperatures. We repair and replace residential water lines for homeowners across Denver, Aurora, Lakewood, Arvada, Littleton, Centennial, Westminster, and the surrounding communities.

Signs You Need This Service

  • High water bills or unexplained water usage
  • Low water pressure throughout the property
  • Wet spots, pooling, or soft ground in the yard
  • Water pooling near the foundation
  • The sound of running water with everything shut off
  • Sinkholes or settling near the service line

Main Water Line Repair & Replacement

When a main water line fails, it affects the entire property's supply. If your line has significant corrosion, repeated leaks, or structural failure, full replacement is often the most cost-effective long-term fix. Frequently Asked Questions

Can you repair a water line without digging up my whole yard?
In most cases, yes. We locate the exact damaged section first, which lets us limit excavation to the area that actually needs work. Where the line allows, we use trenchless methods to further reduce disruption to landscaping and driveways.
Should I repair or replace my water line?
It depends on the line's age, material, and condition. A single isolated failure on an otherwise sound pipe is usually a repair. Repeated leaks, heavy corrosion, or aging galvanized or polybutylene lines often make full replacement the smarter long-term investment. We give you a straight answer after locating the problem.
Do you handle permits and coordination with the water utility?
Yes. Underground water service work often requires permits and coordination with the local water authority. We manage that process so your repair or replacement is completed correctly and to code.
